## Y2 • Week 33
After being recommended more than once as a nice place to visit, some friends and I went to Collioure today. A really pretty seaside village – easy to walk around, quaint streets and nice cafés – it's been added to my places of interest list to take visitors to 🤩. A 1.5 hour drive south almost to the Spanish border with beautiful views of the Pyrenees still covered in snow.

A joke backfired through the week when somebody asked did I drink champagne which I jokingly answered only on days that end in “i” the equivalent to only on days that end in “y” in English. In French though Sunday is dimanche so I had to explain that I didn’t drink Champagne at all – the exception being if a permit residency card ever turns up!

Wind has been truly horrible for the last few weeks, calm now. Birds were loving being pushed and pulled through the sky with some huge seagulls flying in to land in the yard regularly – a direct 10km flight from water.
