## Y1 • Week 40 
Holy mother of Jesus and all the saints combined • my residence permit has been approved 😀. I’m a bit confused and think it’s for only one year • however I now know the process. There are many things I can move forward with once received!

Everything is growing now winter is gonsky. I’ve propagated seeds for months which are now planted, while abstract vegies are prolific appearing magically from many bags of potting mix. I’m thinking these will be treated and not fruit, and if not they’ll be used for compost. Kerbsides and fields are full of poppies and weeds which are all beautiful. And trees have arrived in my street finishing six months roadworks – making it the nicest street in Béziers… might be a little biased 😁

I’ve socialised more than worked this week however floor downstairs is now fully prepped for self-levelling compound which will be followed by all downstairs flooring. Don’t mention the showers which are no closer to being finished but seriously need to be!

Spent a beautiful evening at a tapas bar with friends yesterday, watching the sun go down over the river Orb. Restaurants that have been closed during winter are now opening for summer with gusto and fanfare.
