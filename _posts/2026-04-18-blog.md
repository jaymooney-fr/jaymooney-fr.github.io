## Y2 • Week 35
Local feral cat walked into the kitchen and through wet cement leaving cute paw prints which will be covered with flooring. Spent some time this morning browsing through a nearby market buying food, pasta bowls and new clothes for incoming warmer weather. Most things three euros or less except the meat 😁. Meat and fish are expensive here although I hear Australia is trending the same way. I nearly bought horse meat by accident through the week – same price as beef 😯.

Raised vegie patch has finally been raised to a height I’m happy with, and seedlings are being propagated and planted daily. It all started with one bean and now I have rosemary and a bay leaf bush keeping it company while hoping the coriander lives which will be a first. Orange tree is full of blossoms and fig tree is loaded… sounds a lot like last year I’m sure 🤩.

Having fun showing current aussie visitor around in amongst bits of work. Haven’t been too motivated so need to get my act together again. Having the shower installed has become an exercise in procrastinating. Next week some action hopefully!
