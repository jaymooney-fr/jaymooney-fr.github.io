## Y2 • Week 36
I’m eating better with Australian visitor… meals I wouldn’t bother cooking for myself. And naturally lots of cheese and baguettes. Bought some jeans at the local supermarket reduced from 44€ to 7€. Must have been at the end of the sale because on checking the docket they were reduced by another 20%. Three pairs bought and I’m thinking I’ll head back next week to see if further reduced. We’re heading into summer so I’ll try not to eat too much before I need them.

Tiny birds have built a nest under the verandah on top of an aluminium upright. They would have been better off in the bamboo as I’ve kicked the upright more than once and we’ve had hail on the tin roof. Not sure if they’re still resident but not game to move anything just in case they’ve laid eggs.

I’ve overloaded an Aldi trolley – ball bearings going in all directions. Didn’t think this was possible on a four euro tool… made in Germany 😁. Not dark until 21:00 with the change of season which is beautiful… and the skies are an incredible blue most days… still love living in the Mediterranean ❤️.
