## Y1 • Week 43
I couldn’t make an appointment online to pick up my residence permit (error 404 🤣) so headed into town before lunch parking in Rue d’Austerlitz, easy to remember, bit like Australia. Paid the fee for the permit at a tobacco shop (little bit archaic 😯 but we know the rules) and at council offices made an appointment for later this month.

Friday coffee club friends had booked a restaurant for lunch with a “wear a dress” mandate. Had to wash a dusty dress – first time worn in France! On a hot day, in heals I lost the car! Walked around a few blocks and then sat at a bus stop to work out what to do… a man thinking I was for sale offered money for a kiss and more 🤣 Ended up asking chatgpt to list all the streets in Béziers starting with “Aust”. Five minutes later I was in aircon, alone, heading to lunch… only three of us in dresses and only one with blisters and a good story!

It was tools down for a few days this week while crook… read (listened to) a book about the crusades 1000 years ago, and before bed sores set in, finished off a few cosmetic changes – nothing up a ladder!
