## Y2 • Week 42
Met up with some friends in Lespignan – a little village 20 mins away for the seasonal migration on foot, of livestock to suitable grazing grounds. An annual event where the main street was closed, while the local brass band, horse carriages, goats and sheep streamed past. We then sat in the shade being serenaded with “roll out the barrel” by the band and eating a three course meal including lamb chops… which were truly delicious!

The word for a fullstop in French is le point. A question mark: le point d’interrogation, an exclamation mark: le point d’exclamation, a colon: les deux-points, an ellipse: les points de suspension 🤩.

Bushwalked with a group of amateur photographers today – mobiles only! Nobody understands bushwalking so I refer to hiking, walking in the country, walking in the sun, enjoying the heat – all with my akubra firmly in place 😀.
