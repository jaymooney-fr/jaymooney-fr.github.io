## Y2 • Week 38
More Aussie visitors stopping over for a couple of nights this weekend for a whirlwind tour of Béziers and surrounds. I’ve recently met an Australian lady who sold up and came over to live here permanently… and after two months is thinking of returning • I’m still loving it! In French the word for the first time you meet someone is rencontré – and the word when meeting up with somebody you have met before is retrouve. Look at me giving a lesson 🤣

Most Saturdays I walk to a market for fresh fruit, veg and a baguette – always taking a few coins for some women begging. It benefits all of us as the collection of low denomination coins just grows and grows. There’s no rounding, 1 and 2 cent coins are still in use as are chequebooks – so I’m never in a hurry when shopping where groceries are placed into trolleys and conversations ended before handbags are rummaged through to find wallets.

Weather is currently overcast and drizzly which has brought in the birds. An Aussie friend sent “Birds of France” (book) and together with “Merlin Bird ID” (App on phone) I’m learning who is popping in. The first two were a common wood pigeon and sparrow – I knew I was living with exotic creatures! The phone app updates records of what’s where and with millions of users, changes are being mapped better than ever before.
