## Y2 • Week 37
There is a convention in France for naming dogs and cats for any given year in a 20 year cycle. In 2024 only names beginning with V were authorised, followed by A in 2025. Most breeders follow the convention as well as many pet owners following public consciousness.

Visited a pizza shop for lunch with friends through the week – truly extraordinary… lots of freshly shaved ham, parmesan, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, fresh rocket on a light as air puffed base. It’s not a UNESCO site however all upcoming visitors will be taken to this temple! I heard a joke where a man explains how he overcame the difficulty when ordering a baguette (forgetting if it’s une or un). He says it costs him a bit more but he just orders deux (two) every time 🤣

The Hérault department where I live has some spectacular natural wonders. Grotte de Clamouse (Clamouse caves) is rated the most beautiful concentration of crystals in Europe. Of course I’ve driven past many times without realising this… it’s less than an hour from home and I finally stopped this week for the tour 😁.
