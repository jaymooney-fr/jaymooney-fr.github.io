## Y2 • Week 46
Hilariously I’m applying for a new residence permit and the prefilled data has my maiden name as my married name – which I can’t change online 🤣 Application for everything needs me to supply officially signed birth, marriage, divorce certificates.

Weather continues to be warm – the advantage is mosquitoes are rare and I leave my window open at night. It is truly beautiful watching the moon pass by lighting up my bedroom during the witching hour… while I’m sorting out all sorts of problems 😁 Nice also that it passes Australia with the same frequency!

France V Amazon was a win for France last week concerning a €3 delivery fee imposed on online book orders under €35. France has long cherished bookshops and is fighting for independent stores. The flipside is that Amazon has announced plans to invest more than €15 billion in France between 2026 and 2028, spanning logistics infrastructure, cloud computing and AI development. Which of course will wipe out bookshops for sure!

I try not to mention names, however my sister has complained about the lack of photos posted recently 🤣 It’s been a few unexciting weeks, so more photos promised.
