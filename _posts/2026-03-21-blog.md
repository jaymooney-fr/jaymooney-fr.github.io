## Y2 • Week 31
In French many English sayings are the same with different animals eg I have goose bumps • I have the skin of a chicken, Blind as a bat • Short-sighted as a mole, Quiet as a mouse • Mute as a carp, Frog in my throat • Cat in my throat and It’s raining like a cow urinating!
Spent a full five days in Paris, and my birthday walking through and around the palace and gardens of Versailles. Hesitant to drive again as it is 8-9 hours with breaks and 4½ hours on the fast train.

Relaxed for a few days after returning fighting an ongoing cold – cracking on today though with plastering and placing skirting boards around the studio. Pressure’s on to get the studio finished and rented... and which will stop the feral cat lodging in an upstairs guest bedroom for free. 🤩
