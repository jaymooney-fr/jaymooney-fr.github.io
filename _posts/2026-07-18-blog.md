## Y1 • Week 48
Have just changed current year’s blogs titled Y2 • Week ## to Y1 – looked like I’ve been here nearly three years, however it’s only nearly two. I'm soooo confused!!

This week France celebrated Bastille Day – a Public holiday. Béziers fireworks were cancelled due to drought and fires however buses and garbage trucks remained in full operation! Rubbish is picked up locally on three different days depending on the bin. In the city centre, rubbish is picked up every day. I struggle to understand how to sort and when I ask I’m laughed at by French people who imply nobody cares. 😯

On the public holiday I was happily using electric tools before realising it was not only a public holiday but also school holidays – very quickly became silent!! Rabbit still with me (no clue for pickup date) and minding an old dog again later this week. Nobody has trusted me minding kids yet – probably just as well 😁
