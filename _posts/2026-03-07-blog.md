## Y2 • Week 29
Builder has returned to put in place a beam plus two sheets of corrugated iron – and is now officially finished. It’s been a long year. Sparky is also nearing completion, after some work still needing to be done by me before he can finalise. I’m currently installing (in the studio) a shower, first of three throughout the house • the other two will be easier as I’ll know what I’m doing 😯.

Since the Rugby last weekend I’ve had a cold and hacking cough. Didn’t turn into anything serious and I’m almost back to normal. Weather has been foul, so early to bed learning lots about pruning fruit trees from a book sent from Australia, and youtube (of course)! In the rain a mad group of women and I still ventured out to the newly renovated Théatre des Variétés in Béziers (derelict for over 30 years) and to Nîmes by train. I’m typing now listening to some very happy and noisy birds rummaging through an unkempt garden in light drizzle.

Also this week windy conditions whipped up Sarah desert sands several thousand metres into the air, and brought them over the Pyrénées to the south of France. Already sneezing, I’m not sure which to blame for the sore throat.
