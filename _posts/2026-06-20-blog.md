## Y2 • Week 44
Went for a dip in the Mediterranean for the first time… Portiragnes beach is 17km from home, not built-up and not touristy. Also it’s not tidal, and with no more than 6” swells it’s the ideal setting for a café near the water’s edge. French friends are very unhappy that it is now illegal to smoke on the beach.

Last weekend I held a garden party in amongst the rubble… fancy coffee pot and sandwiches. Picked a ripe fig and sliced it up, just to show off. Fig trees are fast growing and popping up everywhere – initially I was protective • now I just dig them out. I love everything in the garden, however figs and bamboo are my favourites 😀.

Have spent a few days on my stomach and elbows levelling and laying flooring under three steps • This has been the hardest bit with fiddly cutting in and needing to raise the door. At some stage I need to accept my age 😁! All other rooms are basic squares with just the furniture moved in and out as I go.
