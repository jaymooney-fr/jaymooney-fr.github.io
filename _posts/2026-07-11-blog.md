## Y2 • Week 47
I’m minding a rabbit for a neighbour – he’s very cute (the rabbit), lives outside, and after a week I’m allowed to scratch his head. Very skittish, there’s lots of hopping around the yard at speed – odd watching the hop rather than a run🤣. He’s good at hiding, thankfully reappearing both times I thought I’d lost him. Another first for this week was being invited to karaoke at a French couple’s unit. I belted out a tribute to Bonnie Tyler which was awful – ensuring I won’t be invited back 😁. Turns out the pair are professionals!

First Wednesday of the month as per normal, sirens sounded a test at midday. Thursday 9am they sounded and as a few small bushfires are currently within 30km I was alert. Turns out it was someone’s vacuum cleaner. Friday 4am I awoke smelling smoke that wasn’t bushfires – went back to sleep assuming it was a car being vandalised due to the locality I live in 😯

Flooring down in the utility room and tools in place. It is truly amazing needing something and knowing where to find it without the despairing search. Still need to plaster and paint the walls – and eventually will change shelving. Now laying flooring in the lounge and feeling a tipping point with the downstairs flooring then finished.
