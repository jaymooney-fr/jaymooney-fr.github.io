## Y2 • Week 41
Aussie visitor left today after two months of sightseeing in between working. It’s still a pleasure showing off local sights and changing landscapes within an hour from home. May in France has four long weekends the last was Whit Monday – religiously significant and a memory back to 2003 when a severe heatwave led to more than 15000 deaths. Current news is reporting hottest temperatures ever recorded in parts of France this week… so my fan is set to hi max.

Not in my garden but nearby and often I see black and white birds with long tails… looked up the bird book – and it’s a common magpie. One day I’m going to post an exotic find!! The bean in the garden bed is not a bean. I was laughing with a friend that I think it’s a zucchini and she said it could be a courgette… after a minute I realised we were talking about the same thing.

Nicked my knuckle with the angle grinder this week… through a heavy-duty glove. No real damage but worked with one hand all week. A gentle reminder to take care because I haven’t worked out the hospital system yet 🤣
