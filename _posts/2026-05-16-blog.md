## Y1 • Week 39
Through the week I received a second extension to my original one year visa, as the residency permit continues being processed. Everyone I speak to laughs… we’re all experiencing the same delays.

Revisited Collioure (cute village near the Spanish border) for a few nights with current aussie visitor. Came down with a cold but ventured out to see Château d'Aubiry (now a ruin) and Château de Valmy (now a hotel). Both built by a wealthy industrialist in late 1800 for his children – they are crazy opulent with insane gardens.

Got very lost returning home while trying to find Rivesaltes Camp – a memorial paying tribute to where 60,000 were imprisoned or housed as a result of the Spanish Civil War, Second World War, the Holocaust and the Algerian War. Getting lost is never a problem… it happens so often 🤣
