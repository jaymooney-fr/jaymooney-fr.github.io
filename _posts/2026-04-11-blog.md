## Y2 • Week 34
Weather has turned warm 😀. Through the week I took off a jumper leaving one layer… Oh what a feeling! All walls are now up and I’ll tackle tiling and plumbing for showers next week – with another Australian who has turned up. I don’t understand why, but I’m very happy to have company and help again for some big works, in amongst some sight seeing.

One of Béziers' most revered sanctuaries, church of the Madeleine has reopened after renos. The scene of the bloodiest episode in localised history – in 1209 many men, women and children who had taken refuge inside were exterminated.

I read a fb post about the relocation of the prominent and beautiful statue of Paul Riquet… I was saddened and thought it was something I’d march for if came to a vote. It was an April Fool’s joke and I was left feeling hugely relieved.
