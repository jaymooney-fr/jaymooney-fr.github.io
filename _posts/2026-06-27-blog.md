## Y1 • Week 45
Everyone here applying for their residence permit has their own story, and now I have mine… for almost a year it was being processed. Application submitted three months before my first visa ran out Aug 18 last year, and then three times applied for extensions. Price of permits have increased by 25€ to 250€, so I dropped back into a Tobacco shop to pay the shortfall, giving me two receipts. Rocked up to the council building, registered myself, found the cashier and waited for my name to be called. Lady behind the glass opened up an envelope when it was my turn, took out the permit and after 10 minutes she apologised for the computer taking so long. My eye started to twitch as I could see the permit but not reach it. Turns out there was a problem accepting two receipts for the one permit. Cold air was rushing through a small slot under the window separating us… keeping her cool while I was beginning to overheat. Eventually I signed some paperwork, had the permit in my hand and was back on the street, only to discover the new one year permit started Aug 18 last year (not the date processing accepted) and runs out in less than two months.  I’m starting the application process again… residence permits are applied for yearly for five years, after which they are applied for every five years.

Returned home to a floor where the laying was problematic requiring lifting and relaying twice – possibly due to my frustration combined with the heat. Even during the hardest weeks I’m still loving living here 🤩
